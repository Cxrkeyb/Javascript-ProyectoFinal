Mechanical Energy

Is the sum of kinetic energy and potential energy of a body or system. Kinetic energy is the energy of bodies in motion, since it depends on their velocities and masses. Potential energy, on the other hand, is associated with the work of forces that are called conservative, such as the elastic and gravitational forces, which depend on the mass of the bodies and their position and structure.
